DE LA VEGA

Margarita

Mar 21 | Apr 14, 2023
Previous Next

Pure yet embellished, Margarita's work is captivating

The prodigious child first gained world recognition when her work was displayed at The 59th International Art Exhibition in 2017.

Margarita was soon awarded an excellence schoolarship to attend the prestigious Academy of Fine Arts in Bacerlona, Spain.

Her latest collection inspired by the wild of her home country Venezuela, will be exhibited at GECO in Spring.
